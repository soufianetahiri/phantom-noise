Privacy Mode

Opens decoy tabs in a private incognito window so they never touch your real browsing profile (history, cookies, cache).

How to enable incognito access:
  1. Open chrome://extensions in your browser
  2. Find Phantom Noise and click Details
  3. Toggle "Allow in Incognito" on

Without this, the extension falls back to a hidden minimized window with automatic cleanup — still private, but incognito is stronger.

Removes cookies, cache, and history for visited decoy sites after each tab closes. Automatically active when incognito is unavailable.
